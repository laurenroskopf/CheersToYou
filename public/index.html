Milestone Sets

Details

Month to month you can track your little one’s growth with this adorable set.

Use the numbers each month until baby turns one. After that, just add the “I’m” piece and you’ll be able to use the number set year after year on their birthday!

Set includes: Numbers 1-11 and Pennant 7x12 inches

All numbers are white with a colored backing. Pennant is 3-ply so it can be held or simply lay next to your baby for pictures. Numbers adhere by one sided velcro.

For custom colors, please use the Contact form.

This product is intended for decorative purposes only - it is not a toy. Please hang out of the reach of children.

MEET THE MAKER

Hey there! I’m Alice- artist and maker located in Cedarburg, WI.

I create a modern version of a vintage classic- hand-lettered pennants & garlands- made from premium wool blend felts.

Need a unique gift? Decorating for a celebration? Adding a personal touch to a space?

Everything is designed to raise the ordinary to a little “extra”ordinary!

When I’m not behind my sewing machine, I’m busy being a teacher, a traveler, and a mom. I’m always looking for ways to celebrate and cheer for the people I love- so much joy❤️

Cheers to YOU!
