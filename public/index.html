MEET THE MAKER

Hey there! I’m Alice- artist and maker located in Cedarburg, WI.

I create a modern version of a vintage classic- hand-lettered pennants & garlands- made from premium wool blend felts.

Need a unique gift? Decorating for a celebration? Adding a personal touch to a space?

Everything is designed to raise the ordinary to a little “extra”ordinary!

When I’m not behind my sewing machine, I’m busy being a teacher, a traveler, and a mom. I’m always looking for ways to celebrate and cheer for the people I love- so much joy❤️

Cheers to YOU!
