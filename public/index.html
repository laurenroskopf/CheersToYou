MEET THE MAKER

Hey there! Im Alice- artist and maker located in Cedarburg, WI.

I create a modern version of a vintage classic- hand-lettered pennants & garlands- made from premium wool blend felts.

Need a unique gift? Decorating for a celebration? Adding a personal touch to a space?

Everything is designed to raise the ordinary to a little extraordinary!

When Im not behind my sewing machine, Im busy being a teacher, a traveler, and a mom. Im always looking for ways to celebrate and cheer for the people I love- so much joy

Cheers to YOU!
